## What is "Thread"?
Think of it like a mesh network just for your devices. Every light bulb you plug in acts as a signal booster for the next one.
*   **Old Way:** Hub -> Wi-Fi -> Cloud -> Device (Slow)
*   **New Way (Matter/Thread):** Hub -> Device (Instant, Local)
