## The Villain: Vecna's Final Form

Jamie Campbell Bower returns as Vecna (Henry Creel), but he is not alone. The Duffer Brothers have teased a "monster" that makes the Demogorgons look like pets. With the barrier gone, Vecna doesn't need to open gates anymore; he can walk right through. The psychological horror of Season 4 will shift to physical, visceral warfare.

## Cast & Characters
The original gang is back, but the dynamic has shifted.
*   **Eleven (Millie Bobby Brown):** Now fighting without the need to hide her powers.
*   **Hopper (David Harbour):** Back in the US, dealing with the trauma of Russia but ready to lead the defense of Hawkins.
*   **Steve & Nancy:** The love triangle isn't over, but it takes a backseat to survival.
