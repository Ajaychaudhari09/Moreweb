Netflix released **Raat Akeli Hai – The Bansal Murders** on December 19, 2025. This is the sequel to the acclaimed 2020 film *Raat Akeli Hai*, with **Nawazuddin Siddiqui** returning as Inspector Jatil Yadav.

## The Plot
In this sequel, the wealthy Bansal family of Kanpur is found brutally murdered in their mansion. Jatil Yadav unravels a web of greed, betrayal, and long-buried secrets among the relatives while chasing a cunning killer. The tension ramps up with each reveal, and Siddiqui delivers a standout performance.

## Cast & Crew
Directed by **Honey Trehan** (returning from the original), the film’s ensemble includes:
*   **Radhika Apte**
*   **Chitrangda Singh**
*   **Ila Arun**
*   **Revathi**

Like the first movie, the sequel blends family drama with noir-thriller elements.

## Verdict
Twists keep viewers guessing about motives and who can be trusted. The film runs about 2 hours 16 minutes. Early reviews praised Siddiqui’s performance, noting it as “eerie and engrossing,” though critical reception is mixed.
