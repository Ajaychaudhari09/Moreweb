**Mrs. Deshpande** starts as a conventional drama, lulling viewers into a false sense of security. Anjali Deshpande (Tabu) is a respected woman in her Pune colony. She teaches music, hosts kitty parties, and diligently cares for her ailing husband. Then, one ordinary morning, her husband vanishes without a trace.
