The popcorn is popping, the projectors are humming, and Hollywood is ready to unleash its biggest slate in years. 2025 promises to be a year of spectacle. Here are the films you need to mark on your calendar.

## 1. Avatar: The Tides of Ash (December 2025)
**Director:** James Cameron
**Cast:** Sam Worthington, Zoe SaldaÃ±a, Michelle Yeoh

James Cameron returns to Pandora, but this time, we aren't in the water. *The Tides of Ash* introduces the "Ash People," a clan of Na'vi who worship fire.

## 2. The Batman: Part II (October 2025)
**Director:** Matt Reeves
**Cast:** Robert Pattinson, Andy Serkis

Robert Pattinson dons the cowl again in this gritty crime saga. Rumors suggest the introduction of Clayface.

## 3. Superman: Legacy (July 2025)
**Director:** James Gunn

The DC Universe resets with James Gunn at the helm. This is a look at a younger Superman balancing his Kryptonian heritage.

## 4. Minecraft: The Movie (April 2025)
The best-selling video game of all time finally hits the big screen. Starring Jason Momoa.

## 5. Tron: Ares (October 2025)
Decades after the original, we return to the Grid. This film focuses on Ares, a sentient program that crosses over into the real world.
