# 10 New AI Image & Video Tools (Late 2025) — Honest Reviews You Can Use

AI tools now help creators work faster and smarter, not just churn out prompts. Every tool below launched or evolved in 2025 and offers something for video editors, blog writers, and marketers. Each review covers what the tool does, who it helps, real pricing, best use cases, and links to start.

***

## Google Veo (Veo 2 / Veo 3) — Cinematic video generation

Google’s Veo tools sit inside Google AI Studio and Gemini, focused on b-roll, short cinematic clips, and vertical videos. Veo 3 adds audio creation and quick exports for Reels and Shorts. Movement looks real, with good object physics; fast action scenes (running, fights) aren’t always perfect. Use vertical format for social media; horizontal for presentations.

**Pricing:**  
- Free credits included with testing  
- Pro: $19.99/month  
- Ultra: $249.99/month (includes audio, highest resolution)

[Try Google Veo](https://ai.google.com/research/veo)

**Who should try:**  
Creators wanting quick, realistic background footage, or anyone making social content that needs smooth camera moves.

***

## OpusClip — AI clipping and auto-short creation

OpusClip takes a long-form video and automatically creates highlights, social shorts and TikToks. Its algorithms find “hook moments” that grab attention, resize for every platform, add captions, and even emojis. Team and brand features help scale content creation for businesses. Millions of creators and top YouTubers already use it.

**Pricing:**  
- 7-day free trial, includes 90 min video time  
- Free-forever basic plan (60 min/month)  
- Pro: Flexible credit and monthly options (check [details](https://www.opus.pro))

**Who should try:**  
Perfect for YouTube and podcast creators converting long videos to shareable shorts across Instagram, TikTok, YouTube Shorts.

**Comparison:**  
Compared to manual editing, OpusClip saves hours, automates captions, and helps catch viral moments (not just clips with faces).

***

## Runway Gen-4 & Turbo — Clever image/video creation with control

Runway isn’t just a prompt engine—it’s a creative suite for generating and editing both images and videos. Gen-4 keeps styles and characters consistent across scenes, ideal for explainers and animation. The online editor works like a mini Adobe Premiere, with tons of features like video upscaling, restyling, relighting, and collaborative workflow for teams.

**Pricing:**  
- Free basic plan  
- Standard: Starts at $12/month (pay-per-video credit also available)  
- Advanced workflows, commercial use, export options

[Learn more at Runway](https://www.runwayml.com)

**Who should try:**  
Professional editors, animators, and teams needing continuity. Individual creators wanting control beyond just handing prompts to the AI.

**Comparison:**  
Runway covers a broad spectrum (image, storyboarding, virtual try-on, effects)—many tools in one dashboard. No need for separate photo and video apps.

***

## Pika Labs (Pika 2) — Quick creative video outputs

Pika Labs does fast cinematic clips and short video loops, great for daily content and experimentation. Its credit-based system means light users pay less, heavy users get more quality with premium models. Pika Turbo makes quick gifs or concept shots for social.

**Pricing:**  
- Free trial  
- Credits based on quality (Turbo = cheapest, advanced models = more credits per render)

[Check Pika Labs](https://www.pika.art)

**Who should try:**  
Social creators, artists wanting stylized visuals for intros, loops, backgrounds.

***

## Kaiber AI — Best for animated, artistic motion videos

Kaiber takes text, music, or existing images and creates creative, animated video. It’s not built for realism—think music video effects, surreal loops, painted backgrounds. The styles library is deep, and the workflow is quick for most users.

**Pricing:**  
- Free trial  
- Monthly starts at $10; annual plans save more

[See Kaiber](https://www.kaiber.ai)

**Who should try:**  
Musicians, video editors making lyric videos, anyone needing art-driven motion.

***

## Dreamina (CapCut) — Mobile-first, smooth workflow

Dreamina works inside CapCut, letting creators make images and clips on their phone, edit fast, and export direct to social media or vertical video platforms. Daily free credits let users make content every day. Upgrades unlock HD/4K, faster processing, and commercial use.

**Pricing:**  
- Free daily credits  
- Starter: $9.99/month  
- Creator: $19.99/month  
- Pro: $39.99/month (adds video, 4K output)

[Learn more Dreamina](https://dreamina.capcut.com)

**Who should try:**  
Mobile creators, influencers, anyone active on TikTok, Reels, Shorts.

***

## Haiper AI — Free watermark-free image + video generator

Haiper AI is a zero-cost image and video platform built for rapid creative work. Generate banners, thumbnails, loops—no watermark, no fee. Commercial and enterprise users can request special plans for bigger teams.

**Pricing:**  
- Free (most users)  
- Enterprise plan: Custom

[Try Haiper AI](https://haiper.ai)

**Who should try:**  
Budget-conscious creators, anyone experimenting with AI regularly.

***

## Reve AI — Best for brands and text/image placements

Reve AI nails prompts and layout — “put the logo here,” “make the background blue,” and so on. The built-in editor lets you adjust everything, and the output is sharp and brand-safe. Plans scale from solo users to creative agencies.

**Pricing:**  
- Free credits on signup  
- Paid: $9.99/month (starter), up to $49.99/month for high volume

[Try Reve AI](https://reve.art)

**Who should try:**  
Marketers, designers, entrepreneurs building consistent brand visuals.

***

## Krea AI — HD images, instant vectors, live preview

Krea combines photo and vector output in one app, making it good for print, web, branding. You get instant previews, editing as you generate, and can export PNG or SVG. Free plan suits basic users, max plans unlock speed and resolution for full-time designers.

**Pricing:**  
- Free, Basic: $8/month  
- Pro: $28/month (higher quality)  
- Max: $48/month (unlocks everything)

[Visit Krea AI](https://krea.ai)

**Who should try:**  
Web designers, graphic artists, brands needing scalable assets.

***

## Freepik Mystic — Templates + AI for easy visual production

Mystic puts Freepik’s huge template library with AI generation. Non-designers can quickly make headers, posters, carousels, and social images. Great for bulk creation — start with a template, let AI fill images or backgrounds, customize, export.

**Pricing:**  
- Free: Limited exports  
- Premium: $12/month (higher output, commercial license)

[See Freepik Mystic](https://www.freepik.com/ai/)

**Who should try:**  
Daily bloggers, social media managers, students, or anyone wanting quick visuals fast.
