The Amazon Great Indian Festival 2025 starts on September 23 with 24-hour early access for Prime members, featuring deep category-wide discounts, SBI 10% bank offers, and expanded delivery for faster drops across India.[1][2]

### Key dates and highlights
- Main sale: September 23; Prime early access: 24 hours earlier.[2][1]
- Early Deals are live ahead of the sale, letting shoppers grab pre-festival offers before day one.[2]
- Bank offers: 10% instant discount on SBI Credit/Debit Cards and EMI, plus Amazon Pay ICICI 5% unlimited cashback.[2]
- Scale: 17 lakh+ sellers participating; 45 new delivery stations added to speed up deliveries in tier 2 and 3 cities.[1]

### What’s new in 2025
- Smarter shopping with Rufus AI for comparisons, price history, and tailored recommendations to cut decision time.[2]
- Visual discovery via Lens AI, plus AI Review Highlights to summarize thousands of reviews at a glance.[2]
- Faster deliveries through expanded last-mile capacity, new sort/fulfilment centers, and same/next-day coverage in more locations.[1]

### Bank and payments stack to maximize savings
- Stack core savings: product discount + coupon + 10% SBI instant discount + Amazon Pay ICICI cashback + No-Cost EMI where available.[2]
- Use Amazon Pay Later for no-cost EMIs across fashion, furniture, and kitchen appliances, with instant credit up to ₹60,000 for eligible customers.[2]
- Travel via Amazon Pay: up to 20% off flights, 45% off hotels, and 17% off bus bookings; ICICI Pay cardholders get an extra 5% cashback.[2]

### Best times to buy
- Use Prime early access to beat day-one rush, especially for lightning deals and Prime-only “Prime Dhamaka Offers.”[2]
- Watch Early Deals before the sale to secure lowest-ever prices that may not return on day one.[2]
- Expect restocks as delivery capacity has scaled nationwide—don’t panic-buy if a model slips out of stock early.[1]

### Category cheat sheet
- Smartphones and accessories: up to 40% off on leading brands (Apple, Samsung, OnePlus, iQOO, Xiaomi, Realme); combine with SBI instant discount for best net price.[2]
- Laptops, TVs, and home entertainment: up to 45% off on Windows/AI PCs and big-screen TVs, with up to ₹20,000 cashback and long-tenure No-Cost EMI.[2]
- Home appliances: up to 65% off on LG, Samsung, Haier, and Godrej, with exchange and EMI sweeteners.[1]
- Fashion and beauty: 50–80% off across marquee brands, plus time-bound 8 PM Deals and Express Deals.[2]
- Everyday essentials and Fresh: flat cashback (including ₹400), ₹1 and ₹9 deals on Fresh storefront, and up to 70% off on daily-use items.[2]
- Amazon devices: up to 50% off Echo, Kindle, and Fire TV devices, with highlighted discounts on Echo Pop and Fire TV Stick 4K.[2]

### Delivery and coverage
- 45 new delivery stations are live across tier II and III cities to improve speed and availability this festive season.[1]
- Same-day to 50% more cities and next-day to twice as many locations versus last year, thanks to network expansion.[1]
- 12 new fulfilment centers and 6 new sort centers were added, boosting storage and sortation capacity ahead of the sale.[1]

### Pro tips to win deals
- Shortlist 3–4 product options and track price history via Rufus AI before the sale window opens.[2]
- Lock in card eligibility (SBI Credit/Debit) and ensure Amazon Pay ICICI is active to capture extra cashback.[2]
- Plan category buy windows: mobiles and laptops during Early Deals/early access; fashion and beauty during time-bound night windows.[2]

### Travel, business, and Bazaar value
- Book travel during the festival: flights up to 20% off, hotels up to 45% off, buses up to 17% off on Amazon Pay.[2]
- Amazon Business: up to 65% off across electronics, equipment, and office categories with curated gifting catalogues.[2]
- Amazon Bazaar: entry-price storefront with cashback on minimum orders and first-time user bonuses across everyday essentials.[2]

### Quick FAQ
- When does the sale start? September 23; Prime gets 24-hour early access.[1][2]
- Which bank offers are active? 10% instant discount on SBI cards/EMIs, plus Amazon Pay ICICI 5% unlimited cashback.[2]
- Are there pre-sale deals? Yes—Early Deals are live before the main event.[2]
- Will delivery be faster this year? Yes—Amazon added 45 delivery stations and expanded same/next-day coverage.[1]

### Bottom line
The smartest strategy is to combine Prime early access with Early Deals, layer SBI instant discounts and Amazon Pay ICICI cashback, and use Amazon’s new AI tools to verify price history and fit, then check out fast while expanded delivery capacity brings orders quicker across India.[1][2]
